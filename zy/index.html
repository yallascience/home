About

Who We Are

A multidisciplinary team at KFUPM pioneering the science behind sustainable hydrogen economies and next-generation energy storage.

Our Mission

Advancing clean hydrogen and sustainable energy technologies through bold experimental and computational research — from lab-scale insights to global-scale impact.

Affiliation

Department of Physics & IRC-HTCM (Interdisciplinary Research Center for Hydrogen Technologies and Carbon Management), KFUPM, Saudi Arabia.

Approach

We fuse experimental synthesis, electrochemical testing, DFT simulation, multiphysics modeling, and machine learning to accelerate materials discovery and reactor design.

Research

Focus Areas

Six interconnected pillars driving breakthrough discoveries at the frontier of clean energy science.

01

Hydrogen Production & Storage

Steam methane reforming (SMR), water-gas shift (WGS), and advanced storage systems including oil shale hydrogen extraction for domestic scalability.

  • SMR
  • WGS
  • H₂ Storage
  • Oil Shale
02

Pd Membrane Separation

Design and development of palladium-based dense membranes for ultra-pure H₂ separation integrated with reforming reactors, plus YSZ-protected Pd membrane systems.

  • Pd Membranes
  • H₂ Purity
  • YSZ
  • Aramco Collab
03

Electrocatalysis (OER & HER)

Advanced electrocatalysts — FeP, NiP, NiFeP, Co/Mn-oxide systems — for oxygen and hydrogen evolution reactions in both alkaline and acidic media.

  • OER
  • HER
  • FeP/NiP
  • Alkaline/Acidic
04

Next-Gen Batteries

Ni–Zn batteries with composite carbon/NiLDH cathodes. Perovskite-based electrodes, zinc-ion batteries with additive engineering for superior energy density.

  • Ni–Zn
  • Zinc-Ion
  • NiLDH
  • Perovskite
05

DFT Computational Modeling

First-principles DFT calculations using Quantum Espresso & Open-MX for TMD nanostructures, photocatalytic water splitting, and metal-sulfur batteries.

  • Quantum Espresso
  • Open-MX
  • TMDs
  • COMSOL
06

Machine Learning & AI

ML-guided screening of HER electrocatalysts, MATLAB regression, scikit-learn pipelines, and predictive models for quantum capacities of advanced cathode materials.

  • scikit-learn
  • MATLAB
  • HER Screening
  • DFT+ML

Vision

Vision & Future Goals

Building toward a carbon-neutral world powered by clean hydrogen and advanced energy storage.

"We envision a future where clean hydrogen serves as a primary energy source and advanced batteries, reinforced with safe and sustainable materials, drive efficient energy storage — together enabling net-zero chemical industries and a carbon-neutral world."

— Yamani Research Group
🚀

Pilot Hydrogen Production

Develop a catalytic membrane SRM–WGS tandem system and explore domestic oil shale resources as hydrogen sources and storage media.

♻️

Carbon Circularity

Bridge hydrogen production with CO₂/CH₄ utilization to enable sustainable circular energy pathways for the chemical industry.

🔋

Next-Gen Ni–Zn Batteries

Design optimized composite cathodes, establish scalable fabrication methods, and launch pilot trials for renewable and grid applications.
